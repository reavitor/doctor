Orthotics as a Simple, Non-Surgical Solution

Orthotics are inserts that are custom built for your foot. These inserts tailor to your particular problem areas, alleviating pressure and strain while enabling the foot to continue to operate to avoid further injury or imbalanced weakness. Orthotics are a particularly popular solution among athletes, for they allow the injured to continue participating in sports without risking repeat injury to the feet.

When should I worry about my foot pain

If your foot has been swollen for several days, or if you have experienced pain in one or both feet for more than a week, we recommend you pay a visit to the Unruh Spine Centers. More severe health concerns that require quick treatment include signs of infection, persistent pain or numbness, and if you are unable to put weight on your foot.
